Yuan Zeng

Research Associate in Southern University of Science and Technology, Shenzhen, China

She received her Ph. D degree in Electrical Engineering from the Delft University of Technology, Dleft, The Netherlands, in 2015. From 2015 to 2018, she was a Postdoc Researcher at Computer Vision Lab, Delft University of Technology. Since 2018, she is a research associate at Southern University of Science and Technology. Her main research interests lie in Computer Vision and Communication Signal Processing, including Image Inpainting/Synthesis, Signal/Object Detection and Cooperative Sensing/Distributed Signal Processing, etc.
